# Data Retention

Mission history default: saved until user deletion. Planned choices: delete after completion, 30 days, one year, or account deletion. Uploaded files default: 30 days with earlier deletion available.

Users must be able to request account deletion, mission-history deletion, upload deletion, export, correction, and restriction. Limited records may be retained for legal obligations, fraud prevention, disputes, accounting, or security. Production implementation requires backend deletion verification, backup expiry, processor propagation, and audit records.
