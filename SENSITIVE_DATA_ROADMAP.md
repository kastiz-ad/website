# Sensitive Data Roadmap

Sensitive profile, document vault, external autofill and provider sharing remain disabled.

Before passport/visa vaults, payment-token references, emergency contacts, accessibility/health information, government-form preparation or provider autofill can launch, Kastiz requires: authenticated backend sessions, encryption in transit and at rest, per-user row access controls, secrets management, audit logs, malware-scanned private uploads, expiring document links, deletion/export workflows, retention rules, incident response, access reviews, Korean legal review and explicit founder approval.

Payment details should be processor tokens only; raw cards and bank credentials must never enter the application profile.
