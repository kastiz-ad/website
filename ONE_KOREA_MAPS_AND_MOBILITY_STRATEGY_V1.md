# ONE Korea Maps and Mobility Strategy V1

Kakao Local and Naver Search/Maps are candidate place sources; Kakao Mobility, Naver Cloud directions, authorized transit sources, and official pages are capability-specific sources. No provider is assumed to supply ratings, reviews, hours, menus, reservations, traffic, transit, taxi availability, or dispatch unless separately verified.

ONE can rank a sourced ONE Pick and two alternatives by mission fit, preferences, dietary/accessibility needs, price, distance, verified route/time, opening/reservation state, atmosphere, parking/transit, review quality/quantity/recency, and source trust. Missing ratings are never invented.

Taxi route, estimate, handoff, availability, dispatch, and payment are distinct. Alpha can prepare pickup/destination and a handoff, but dispatch remains `PARTNERSHIP_REQUIRED`/disabled.
