# ONE Mission Completeness Engine V1

ONE expands the desired human outcome into only the categories relevant to purpose, destination, duration, participants, relationship, age, budget, interests, mobility, diet, energy, style, weather, history, culture, safety, accessibility, live-data availability, and approvals. Unstated values are explicit assumptions, never fabricated preferences.

The engine ranks categories, detects gaps, creates balanced experience blocks, and validates experiences, pacing, logistics, meals, rest, reservations, weather alternatives, safety/return planning, alternatives, and constraints. Multi-day plans with transaction categories alone fail the quality gate and may retry once before transparent limited mode.

Long-duration goals are delegated to the Life Pathway Engine, which extends completeness into eligibility, qualifications, documents, milestones, dependencies, responsible parties, recovery and completion criteria.
