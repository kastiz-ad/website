# ONE Real-World Action Classification V1

Every step is classified as `ONE_CAN_COMPLETE`, `ONE_CAN_PREPARE`, `USER_MUST_COMPLETE`, `PROVIDER_REQUIRED`, `GOVERNMENT_REQUIRED`, `IN_PERSON_REQUIRED`, `IDENTITY_VERIFICATION_REQUIRED`, `APPROVAL_REQUIRED`, `INFORMATION_REQUIRED`, or `UNAVAILABLE`.

Legally personal actions are never represented as completed by ONE. Booking, payment, submission, provider contact, insurance purchase, bank opening and other irreversible actions are unavailable in V1.
