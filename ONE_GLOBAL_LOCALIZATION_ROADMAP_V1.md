# ONE Global Localization Roadmap V1

Future interface locales are ranked by user demand, launch market, mission volume, revenue potential, providers, legal/payment readiness, support capacity, and reviewed localization quality. Candidate languages include Japanese, Simplified and Traditional Chinese, French, Portuguese, German, Italian, and Arabic; this is not a launch commitment. Conversation support may precede interface localization.

Each launch requires complete resource parity, native-language review, accessibility review, safety/legal equivalence, provider-region QA, and device/browser testing.
