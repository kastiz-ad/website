# Intelligence Privacy V1

## Consent boundary

ONE learns only from approved and permitted data. Derived patterns are stored apart from raw mission content and carry consent scope, source, expiration and status. Learning can be paused or cleared. Browser storage is demo-only and is not a secure production authority.

ONE never unexpectedly infers pregnancy, religion, politics, sexual activity, financial distress, relationships, legal problems or medical conditions. Church/community information requires explicit user supply or a future consented connection.

## Shared workspaces

Suggestions require active workspace membership plus the appropriate schedule/location permissions. Private member memory is never exposed. Child information is rejected unless it is consented, appropriate logistics data. Safe language says a shared time “appears to work,” not where a specific person will be at an exact time.

## Non-creepy language

Use “Would this help?”, “ONE can prepare this,” or “Your upcoming plan may need preparation.” Never say “we noticed you always,” “we have been watching,” or reveal detailed tracking.

## Production requirements

Production needs server-authoritative consent and membership checks, encrypted storage, retention/deletion jobs, auditability, child/privacy review, source verification, user-facing per-pattern removal and independent security/privacy assessment before external adapters are connected.
