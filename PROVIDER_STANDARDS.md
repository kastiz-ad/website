# Provider Standards

Allowed status labels are: Recommended, Partner, Identity Verified, License Verified, Background Checked, Community Reviewed, Prototype Profile, Publicly Sourced, and Unverified.

Never use “Verified” alone. Each evidence-based status requires its source, scope, checked date, expiry where relevant, and re-check procedure. Prototype profiles must not imply a real person or commercial relationship. Recommendations are product rankings, not identity, license, or safety verification.

High-trust categories—childcare, tutoring, legal, healthcare, finance, accommodation, and insurance—require specific risk disclosures and user-side verification checklists before any provider contact or approval.
