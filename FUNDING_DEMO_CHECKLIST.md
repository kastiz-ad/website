# Funding Demo Checklist

- [ ] Fresh browser session with intended theme/language
- [ ] One travel and one non-travel mission
- [ ] Customization, estimates, provider status, and approval protection
- [ ] Prototype limitations and no-real-booking disclosure
- [ ] Business, safety, privacy, pricing, roadmap, and early-access pages
- [ ] Live-API/fallback explanation and known limitations
- [ ] Mobile and desktop presentation routes
- [ ] No private tabs, credentials, or personal files visible
- [ ] Founder narrative, market hypothesis, validation plan, use of funds, and milestones without invented traction
