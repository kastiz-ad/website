# ONE Approval Invalidation V1

Material changes clear prior authorization and set `APPROVAL_REVIEW_REQUIRED`: total cost, merchant, provider, date/time/location, participants, reservations/tickets, transportation, payment/cancellation terms, information sharing, external communication, or irreversible action.

Display language, formatting, expanded explanation, and non-material notes preserve approval only when the underlying authorized action is byte-for-byte equivalent. Every summary explains whether and why approval was cleared. Booking, payment, taxi dispatch, provider contact, sending, and submission remain disabled.

Pathway changes to eligibility, provider, legal route, documents, schedule, participants, price, location, external communication or irreversible actions require `APPROVAL_REVIEW_REQUIRED`. No V1 pathway executes externally.
