# Launch Checklist

- [ ] Business registration and public business details
- [ ] Domain email routing and outbound authentication
- [ ] Named support channel and response expectations
- [ ] Qualified Korean privacy and terms review
- [ ] Payment-provider, fee, tax, and PCI-scope review
- [ ] Refund/cancellation process and third-party terms display
- [ ] Production authentication; account deletion and data export
- [ ] Provider verification evidence, expiry, and complaints
- [ ] Emergency handling and safety reporting
- [ ] Incident response owners, exercise, and processor contacts
- [ ] Accessibility keyboard/screen-reader/contrast review
- [ ] Analytics consent, minimization, retention, and verification
- [ ] Backups and restore test; security and secret review
- [ ] Mobile, tablet, desktop, and no-overflow testing
- [ ] English/Korean human review and legal counsel review
- [ ] Customer onboarding and early-user recruitment
- [ ] Partner outreach and grant demo preparation
