# Approval Policy

Explicit approval is required before booking, purchase, payment, reservation, signature, submission, sending personal data to a provider, accepting provider terms, starting a regulated service, or creating a legal obligation.

The final panel must show the selected action, provider, total or estimate, known taxes/fees, cancellation/refund terms, personal data shared, material risks, and a final confirmation. Approval must be specific, informed, affirmative, and recorded by a production backend before real execution.

The current frontend simulates committed actions. It must not be represented as a completed reservation, payment, filing, insurance purchase, or regulated service.
