# Investor demo — 10 minutes

URL: `https://kastiz.com/?demo=1`

1. Homepage (45s): “Google searches. AI answers. Kastiz ONE completes missions.” Explain the visible approval protection.
2. Enter `Plan my Japan trip.` (30s). Complete destination/date/traveler/budget follow-up.
3. Loading (45s): explain that public weather/currency/country sources are queried and failures fall back safely.
4. Results (3m): distinguish live public data from prototype flight/hotel recommendations; show itinerary, visa resource, emergency guidance and budget.
5. Customize (1m): change one provider and remove one optional service.
6. Approval (1m): point out costs, assumptions, risks and the no-execution protection. Approve the simulation.
7. Completion (45s): show the prepared summary; emphasize that no booking/payment occurred.
8. Return home (20s).
9. Enter `Find me an English tutor.` (1m): show different questions and tutor-specific cards.
10. Close (1m): explain early access, disabled payments/authentication and the staged roadmap.
