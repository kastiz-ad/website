# ONE Model Routing V1

The internal policy selects `LIGHTWEIGHT` for detection/classification/short rewriting, `STANDARD` for ordinary missions and drafting, `ADVANCED_REASONING` for complex coordination and conflicting constraints, and `SAFETY_REVIEW` for medical, legal, financial, identity, payment, and government workflows. Model identifiers and reasoning effort are environment configuration and are not exposed to normal users.

Routing does not lower approval or safety requirements. A safety-tier response remains preparation until appropriate human, provider, legal, or specialist review.
