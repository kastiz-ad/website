# Privacy Data Inventory

Potential data: name, email, phone, approximate location, precise GPS with permission, mission text, preferences, uploaded files, travel details, processor-hosted payment data, required passport/visa information, optional emergency contacts, service history, and mission history.

Default rules: collect only what the current mission needs; approximate location first; precise GPS after permission; no payment-card storage in frontend code; emergency contact only with explicit consent; separate marketing consent; restrict sensitive uploads; document every processor and transfer.

Potential processors, only when actually connected: Cloudflare, GitHub, OpenAI, Google, Apple, Kakao, Supabase, AWS, Azure, Korean service providers, and future approved vendors.
