# ONE Experience Intelligence V1

ONE now reviews a prepared mission before asking the user to approve it. The review explains why an idea fits, presents contextual alternatives, and lets the user apply a revision with one click or write a natural-language change.

## Product behavior

- The homepage remains intentionally empty below the mission bar.
- Results follow: prepared plan → ONE review → user revision → approval.
- ONE speaks first; the revision field is never presented without guidance.
- Suggestions are derived from mission context, relationship cues, budget, and consented device-local memory.
- Memory-off means no mission memory is read.
- Suggestions never claim live availability, schedules, prices, ratings, or bookings.
- Approval and execution protections remain unchanged.

## Internal intelligence

- 17 emotion dimensions guide tone and activity selection but are not exposed as scores.
- The activity library contains 160 tagged experience variants.
- Shopping supports Luxury, Mixed, Outlet, Vintage, Street, Markets, Designer, Beauty, Tech, Sports, Anime, Kids, Home, and Local crafts.
- Alternative plan directions include adventure, relaxed, and romantic. Family context changes the reasoning automatically.

No new external API, provider action, booking, purchase, payment, contact, or commitment was added.
