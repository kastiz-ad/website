# ONE Multi-Model Provider Strategy V1

OpenAI remains the default production cognition and language provider. Founder-only configuration may enable Gemini for synthetic investor demonstrations after credentials and privacy review. DEMO provides deterministic offline output. The provider cannot switch silently during a mission and every revision records its producer.

Keys remain server-side. Unconfigured or unreviewed optional providers fail visibly to DEMO; real private data is never sent to a testing provider without explicit founder configuration and privacy approval.
