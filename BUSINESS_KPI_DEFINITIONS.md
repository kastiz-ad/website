# Business KPI Definitions

- Visitor: consented pseudonymous browser identifier with at least one page view.
- Session: one browser-tab session identifier.
- New/returning visitor: first consented visit versus a later consented visit.
- Mission started/completed: accepted mission submission versus completed follow-up.
- Approval intent/confirmed: Make It Reality click versus approval after information review.
- Simulated execution completed: prototype sequence finished; never a real transaction.
- Active/returning user: visitor with mission activity / later consented visit.
- Conversion rate: later funnel count divided by eligible earlier-stage count.
- Retention rate: eligible users returning in a defined period.
- Error/provider success rate: error or successful provider events divided by relevant attempts.
- Early-access signup: completed early-access request event.
- Paid customer and revenue: placeholders only; unavailable until real payment records exist.
