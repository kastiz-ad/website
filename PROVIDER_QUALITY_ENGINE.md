# Provider Quality Engine

The engine normalizes available factors to 0–100 and supports Cheapest, Best Value, Fastest, Highest Quality and Balanced modes. Factors include total cost, refundability, reliability, rating, location, travel time and user preference. Taxes, hidden fees, breakfast, luggage, review count, freshness and loyalty benefits should be normalized by connectors when available.

Missing values are excluded from the denominator and displayed as “Not available from this provider.” Every score returns its inputs and top reasons. A score must never imply live availability; commercial demonstration offers remain clearly simulated.
