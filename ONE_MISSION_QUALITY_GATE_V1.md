# ONE Mission Quality Gate V1

Multi-day leisure missions must include meaningful experiences, pacing, travel logistics, meals, rest, relevant reservation checks, weather dependency/backups, safety/return planning, alternatives, and user constraints. Transaction-only plans, repetitive days, generic destination filler, duplicate content, and placeholders fail. ONE may retry once, then must return transparent limited mode.

Multi-step missions must include a specific desired outcome, at least three meaningful tasks, a recommendation rationale, a constraint or assumption, and a next action. Empty placeholders, duplicate tasks and unsupported success claims fail validation.

Model output is sanitized and schema-validated. A failed response may retry once; a second failure returns a labeled limited-mode result rather than false intelligence. Mission Coin eligibility remains false until completion conditions have verified evidence.
