# Privacy Policy — Draft

Requires professional Korean/PIPA and international legal review before launch. Kastiz ONE should collect only account, chosen profile preferences, mission content, approvals, consent, and privacy-safe security records needed to provide the service. It must disclose purposes, processors, cross-border transfers, retention, user rights, contact methods, and optional analytics choices before production. Raw card/CVV data, identity scans, health information, child information, and secrets are not ordinary Secure Backend V1 fields.
