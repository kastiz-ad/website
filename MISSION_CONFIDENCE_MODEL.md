# Mission Confidence Model

Confidence is a readiness score, not legal, visa, health or safety certainty. Weighted evidence covers required answers, destination, weather, currency, provider freshness, budget, entry-source presence, user-confirmed passport validity, transport, availability, cancellation terms and approval completeness.

Verified evidence receives full weight. Simulated evidence receives 35%, stale evidence 25%, and missing or conflicting evidence 0%. The UI must always show both “Ready” and “Needs attention” lists.
