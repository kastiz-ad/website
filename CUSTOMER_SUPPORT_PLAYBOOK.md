# Customer Support Playbook

1. Identify request type: product help, access, privacy, billing, provider issue, safety, security, or legal.
2. Acknowledge without promising an outcome or response deadline that operations cannot meet.
3. Never request passwords, full card data, or unnecessary passport/medical information.
4. For privacy requests, verify identity proportionately and record deletion/export/correction/restriction scope.
5. For provider disputes, preserve evidence and state that the provider's terms may control cancellation/refunds.
6. For immediate danger, direct the user to local emergency services; ONE is not an emergency-monitoring service.
7. Escalate security reports and sensitive safety reports to their assigned owners.
8. Close with the action taken, unresolved items, and safe next step.
