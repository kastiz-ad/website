# Partnership Requirements

Before a provider is labeled Partner or enabled for execution, document legal identity, service scope, jurisdiction, authorization/license where relevant, data-processing role, security, API authentication, pricing/fees, taxes, cancellation/refund terms, complaints, availability, support escalation, insurance where appropriate, status evidence/expiry, termination, and audit rights.

Childcare, legal, healthcare, finance, accommodation, and insurance require category-specific due diligence. A commercial relationship does not equal identity, license, background, or safety verification.
