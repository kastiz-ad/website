# ONE Payment Rails Strategy V1

Prepared rails: Toss Payments, KakaoPay, Naver Pay, Stripe, PayPal, and applicable Apple/Google payment systems. OpenAI explains costs, compares charges, detects risks, explains refunds, and prepares approval. The payment provider alone authorizes, captures, settles, cancels, refunds, and verifies transactions.

Before final approval show merchant, items, quantity, total, currency, tax/fees, cancellation and refund terms, provider, shared information, and irreversible consequences. All payment adapters are disabled in this sprint; no keys, captures, refunds, or transactions are enabled.
