# ONE Destination Experience Registry V1

The configuration registry contains travel core, travel experience, work/business, and family categories. The current registry has more than 90 categories and declares live-data and approval requirements. It is never rendered as a giant checklist; relevance ranking exposes only meaningful categories.

Destination planning considers signature experiences, memorability, advance reservations, weather, conflicts, geographic grouping, fatigue, backups, and exclusions. Pacing profiles are Relaxed, Balanced, Active, Intensive, Family, Romantic, Business, and Nightlife.
