# Early Access Operations

Supported planning categories are defined in `SERVICE_REGISTRY.md`. Unsupported or high-risk requests receive a clear limitation and safe next step. Live public data may fail; estimates are labeled. Committed actions remain simulated.

Intake routes include early-access request, feedback, bug report, safety report, partner interest, and provider interest. Current forms store a minimal local receipt only and clearly state no external submission service is connected.

Before inviting users, connect a secure form backend, publish response expectations, assign support/safety/privacy owners, prepare onboarding consent, and establish an approved-user list and feedback cadence.
